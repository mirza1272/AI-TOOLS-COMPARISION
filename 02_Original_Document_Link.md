Original Paper Used for Summarization Task
==========================================

Title:   Attention Is All You Need
Authors: Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit,
         Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, Lukasz Kaiser, Illia Polosukhin
Year:    2017
Venue:   NeurIPS 2017
arXiv:   1706.03762

Links:
- Abstract page: https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762
- PDF (free):    https://arxiv.org/pdf/1706.03762

Note:
The full PDF is freely available on arXiv. The paper is not included
in this submission folder to avoid copyright issues. The link above
gives direct access to the PDF.

Why this paper was chosen:
This paper introduced the Transformer architecture, which is the
foundation of all modern LLMs including GPT, Claude, and Gemini.
It is publicly accessible and well-known, which makes it a fair and
neutral choice for comparing summarization quality across models.
