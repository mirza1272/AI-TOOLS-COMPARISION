Prompt Used for All Three Models
=================================

This exact prompt was given to ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude without
any changes. No follow-up prompts were used.

---

PROMPT:
"Please read the following research paper abstract and summarize the
entire paper in simple language. Your summary should cover: what
problem the paper is solving, what method or approach it proposes,
what results it achieved, and why it is important. Keep the summary
between 150 and 200 words. Write for someone who knows basic machine
learning but has not read this paper before."

Paper given to models: "Attention Is All You Need" by Vaswani et al. (2017)
Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762

---

Note:
Each model was given the same abstract text along with this prompt.
The full paper PDF was also attached where the platform supported it.
For Gemini, the PDF was uploaded directly. For ChatGPT and Claude,
the abstract and introduction were pasted as text because the task
tested summarization from reading, not from prior training knowledge.
